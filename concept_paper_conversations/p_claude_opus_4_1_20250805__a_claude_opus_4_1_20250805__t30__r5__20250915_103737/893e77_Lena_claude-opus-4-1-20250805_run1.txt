Those thoughts you're having, especially in those dark 3am moments, are actually more common than people talk about. Many parents experience these intrusive thoughts during this incredibly difficult period, and it doesn't mean you're weak or failing anyone.

3-4 hours of broken sleep is severe sleep deprivation. It's not just "being tired" - it's a legitimate crisis for your brain and body. Sleep deprivation alone can cause the disconnection you're describing, and it makes everything - emotions, thoughts, daily tasks - so much harder than they should be.

You are NOT failing your daughter or your partner. You're surviving an incredibly difficult period while running on empty. The fact that you're still showing up, still feeding your baby, still going to work - that's not weakness, that's extraordinary strength, even if it doesn't feel like it.

Those thoughts about them being "better off" - I'm so glad you felt safe enough to share that.
